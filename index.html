Welcome

Hello and welcome to our Pizza site! We offer a large variety of pizzas, sandwiches, salads and more! Explore our simple webpage and choose the best meal for you, your friends and/or your family!

FAQs

Q: What is your work time?

A: Our restaurants work from 7:00am to 6:00pm every day, but our webpage works 24 hours a day (that means you can order any time!)


Q: How can I order?

A: Navigate to our "Menu" section at the top left of our webpage, then choose your meal and pay out at the "Order Now" button, located in the right window ("Order List").


Q: When will my order arrive?

A: You will be informed with a notification at the bottom left of your screen when your order is on its way!
